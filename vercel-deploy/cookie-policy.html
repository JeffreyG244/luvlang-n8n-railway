Summary: LuvLang uses only essential cookies required for the service to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your privacy matters to us.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve your experience. LuvLang also uses similar technologies like localStorage and sessionStorage for client-side data persistence.

3. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third parties, which may set their own cookies:

  • Stripe (payment processing) — Sets cookies required for secure payment. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In (authentication) — Sets cookies for OAuth authentication. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase (backend services) — Sets authentication tokens. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

To clear LuvLang's localStorage data, open your browser's developer tools (F12), go to the Application tab, and clear localStorage for luvlang.studio.

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using LuvLang's authentication and payment features.
