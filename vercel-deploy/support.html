Common Questions

  • How long does mastering take? Most tracks are processed and ready for download within minutes of upload.
  • What file formats are supported? We accept WAV, MP3, FLAC, AIFF, and OGG files for upload. Mastered files are delivered as high-quality WAV.
  • Can I get a refund? Yes. If you're not satisfied with your mastered track, contact us within 14 days of purchase. See our Refund Policy for details.
  • What's the difference between tiers? Basic provides essential loudness and clarity. Advanced adds multiband processing and stereo enhancement. Premium delivers full analog-modeled mastering with the highest quality settings.
  • I didn't receive my file. Check your browser's downloads folder. If the issue persists, email us with your order details and we'll resend it.
