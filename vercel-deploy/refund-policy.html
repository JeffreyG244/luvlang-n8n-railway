Refund Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Our Promise: If you're not satisfied with the quality of your mastered audio, we'll make it right. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our mastering service. If you are genuinely dissatisfied with the quality of your mastered track, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • Your request must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase
  • You must provide a brief explanation of why the mastering quality did not meet your expectations
  • The purchase must be associated with your LuvLang account

3. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email support@luvlang.studio with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email address and the date of purchase
  3. Briefly describe why the mastering quality was unsatisfactory
  4. We will review your request within 2 business days

4. Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Method: Refunded to the original payment method (credit/debit card via Stripe)
  • Timeline: Refunds are initiated within 2 business days of approval. Your bank may take an additional 5-10 business days to reflect the credit on your statement.
  • Amount: Full purchase price of the mastering service

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be granted in the following cases:

  • Requests made more than 7 days after purchase
  • Issues caused by the source audio quality (e.g., heavily clipped, corrupted, or extremely low-quality recordings)
  • Dissatisfaction with features that are clearly documented in the tier description
  • Repeated refund requests suggesting abuse of the policy
Important: LuvLang processes audio in your browser. We do not have access to your original files. If you experience technical issues during mastering, please contact us before requesting a refund so we can help troubleshoot.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  • Reply to the refund decision email with additional details for reconsideration
  • File a dispute through your payment provider (Stripe/your bank)
  • Contact us at legal@luvlang.studio for formal dispute resolution

7. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Refund requests: support@luvlang.studio
  • Legal inquiries: legal@luvlang.studio
