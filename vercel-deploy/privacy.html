Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data, never sell your information, and your audio files are yours. We use industry-standard security to protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use LuvLang, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account or make a purchase
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe - we never see or store your full card number
  • Audio Files: Files you upload for mastering (processed in your browser, not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them)
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics like page views and feature usage to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process your audio mastering requests
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send important service updates (not marketing spam)

3. Audio File Privacy

Your creative work is yours:

  • Audio processing happens in your browser using Web Audio API
  • We do not listen to, analyze for content, or claim any rights to your audio
  • Files are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save a project
  • We will never use your audio for training AI models or any other purpose

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider
  • Supabase: Our database provider
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

5. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure, encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to user data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Keeping you logged in
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics (privacy-respecting)

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

8. Children's Privacy

LuvLang is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our website.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at:

Email: privacy@luvlang.studio
