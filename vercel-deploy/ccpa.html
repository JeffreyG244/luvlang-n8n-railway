California Privacy Notice

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Summary: If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). LuvLang does not sell or share your personal information for advertising purposes.

1. Your Rights Under the CCPA/CPRA

As a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Right to Limit Use: Limit the use of sensitive personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

2. Information We Collect

In the past 12 months, LuvLang has collected the following categories of personal information:

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the audio mastering service
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To communicate with you about your account and purchases
  • To improve the service and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information.
LuvLang has not sold personal information of California consumers in the preceding 12 months and does not intend to do so. We also do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We share personal information with the following categories of service providers for business purposes only:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (receives billing info for transaction processing)
  • Supabase — Authentication and database services (receives account info)
  • Vercel — Website hosting (receives standard server logs)
  • Google — OAuth authentication (receives authentication tokens)

6. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of your CCPA/CPRA rights, you may:

  • Email us at privacy@luvlang.studio with subject "CCPA Request"
  • Include your full name and the email address associated with your LuvLang account
  • Specify which right(s) you wish to exercise

We will verify your identity before processing your request. Verification may require you to confirm your email address and provide additional information to match our records.

Response Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 10 business days of receiving your request
  • Fulfillment: Within 45 calendar days (may extend to 90 days with notice for complex requests)

7. Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a CCPA request on your behalf. The agent must provide proof of authorization (signed permission or power of attorney) and we may still verify your identity directly.

8. Children's Privacy

LuvLang does not knowingly collect personal information from consumers under 16 years of age. If we learn that we have collected information from a minor under 16, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this California Privacy Notice periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

For California privacy inquiries:
