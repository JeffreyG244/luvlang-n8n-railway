- **When to adjust:** Too bright or too dark

## üí° PRO TIPS

### **1. Let AI Fix It First**
- Don't adjust sliders before AI Upload Scan completes
- Let AI fix clipping automatically
- Only tweak after if you want further changes

### **2. Use A/B Comparison**
- Click "BYPASS" to hear original
- Click again to hear mastered
- Make sure you're improving, not just making it louder

### **3. Watch for Over-Correction**
- If track sounds dull after AI fix, boost highs slightly
- If track sounds thin after AI fix, boost bass slightly
- Small adjustments (+1 to +2 dB) are usually enough

### **4. Trust the Meters**
- **True Peak:** Should be below -1.0 dBTP (streaming standard)
- **LUFS:** -14 LUFS for Spotify/YouTube, -16 for Apple Music
- **Phase Correlation:** Above 0.0 (negative = phase issues)
